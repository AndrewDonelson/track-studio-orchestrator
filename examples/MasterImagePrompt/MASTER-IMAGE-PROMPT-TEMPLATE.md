# TrackStudio Master Image Prompt Template

## Overview
This template generates high-quality, cinematic, photorealistic images with **absolutely no text, letters, or characters**.

---

## Core Prompt Structure

### Base Prompt Formula

```
[SCENE_DESCRIPTION], [LOCATION_DETAILS], [SUBJECT_DETAILS], [LIGHTING], [ATMOSPHERE], [COLOR_PALETTE], [CAMERA_DETAILS], [QUALITY_MODIFIERS], [NEGATIVE_PROMPT]
```

---

## Template Components

### 1. Scene Description (Required)
**What's happening in the image**

Examples:
- "Beach sunset scene with silhouetted figure"
- "Intimate nightclub interior with ambient lighting"
- "Dramatic mountain landscape at golden hour"
- "Urban street corner on rainy evening"
- "Tropical paradise with crystal clear water"

### 2. Location Details (Required)
**Specific setting and environment**

Examples:
- "Miami beach at sunset, palm trees in background"
- "New York City rooftop overlooking skyline"
- "Secluded mountain lake surrounded by pine forest"
- "Neon-lit Tokyo street corner at night"
- "Desert highway stretching to horizon"

### 3. Subject Details (if applicable)
**People, objects, or focal points**

Examples:
- "Beautiful woman in flowing dress, wind in hair"
- "Muscular silhouette against setting sun"
- "Couple embracing on beach, backs to camera"
- "Lone figure walking through mist"
- "Empty chair facing ocean view"

**Key Rules**:
- No faces looking directly at camera (unless required)
- Silhouettes and back-shots work great
- Keep subjects anonymous and timeless

### 4. Lighting (Required)
**Light source, direction, and quality**

Examples:
- "Golden hour sunlight streaming through clouds"
- "Soft rim lighting from behind subject"
- "Moody blue hour twilight illumination"
- "Dramatic volumetric light rays through fog"
- "Warm amber street lights reflecting on wet pavement"
- "Natural window light with soft shadows"
- "Neon signs casting colorful reflections"

### 5. Atmosphere (Required)
**Mood and environmental conditions**

Examples:
- "Romantic and dreamy atmosphere"
- "Melancholic and introspective mood"
- "Energetic and vibrant feeling"
- "Mysterious and ethereal ambiance"
- "Serene and peaceful environment"
- "Intense and dramatic tension"
- "Nostalgic and warm sentiment"

### 6. Color Palette (Required)
**Dominant colors and color grading**

Examples:
- "Warm oranges and deep purples in sky"
- "Cool blues and teals with cyan highlights"
- "Vibrant sunset colors - gold, pink, orange"
- "Desaturated earth tones with muted greens"
- "High contrast blacks and warm amber tones"
- "Pastel pinks and soft lavenders"
- "Rich jewel tones - emerald and sapphire"

### 7. Camera Details (Required)
**Technical photography aspects**

Examples:
- "Shot with 50mm lens, shallow depth of field"
- "Wide angle 24mm perspective, expansive view"
- "Telephoto compression, blurred background bokeh"
- "Low angle looking up, dramatic perspective"
- "Eye level composition, centered subject"
- "Aerial view from above, bird's eye perspective"
- "Macro close-up with selective focus"

### 8. Quality Modifiers (Required)
**Professional photography attributes**

Always include:
- "Professional photography"
- "8K resolution"
- "Highly detailed"
- "Sharp focus"
- "Cinematic composition"
- "Film grain" (optional)
- "Award-winning photography"

### 9. Negative Prompt (CRITICAL)
**What to absolutely avoid**

**Always include**:
```
text, letters, words, typography, watermark, signature, logo, brand names, 
writing, captions, subtitles, title, credit, copyright notice, numbers, 
symbols, alphabet, characters, ui elements, overlays, labels, tags, 
ugly, blurry, low quality, distorted, deformed, disfigured, cartoon, 
anime, CGI, artificial, fake, amateur, pixelated, grainy, noisy
```

## Mood-Based Variations

### Romantic/Passionate
```
Soft focus, warm glow, intimate framing, golden hour or twilight lighting, 
flowing fabrics, gentle movement, dreamy bokeh, pastel or warm color palette, 
shallow depth of field, tender atmosphere
```

### Energetic/Upbeat
```
Vibrant colors, dynamic composition, motion blur elements, bright lighting, 
saturated color palette, wide angle perspective, action-oriented, lively 
atmosphere
```

### Melancholic/Sad
```
Desaturated colors, overcast lighting, cool color palette with blues and greys, 
rain or mist, isolated subjects, empty spaces, moody atmosphere, film grain
```

### Mysterious/Dark
```
Low key lighting, deep shadows, selective illumination, dark color palette, 
fog or smoke elements, dramatic contrast, noir aesthetic, atmospheric haze
```

### Peaceful/Serene
```
Soft natural lighting, balanced composition, pastel or muted colors, calm 
atmosphere, minimal elements, wide open spaces, gentle colors, tranquil mood
```

## Technical Quality Standards

### Resolution & Detail
- Always specify "8K resolution" or "ultra high resolution"
- Include "highly detailed" or "ultra detailed"
- Add "sharp focus" or "tack sharp"

### Professional Quality
- "Professional photography"
- "Award-winning photography"
- "Commercial photography quality"
- "Magazine cover quality"

### Camera Terminology
- Lens focal length (16mm, 24mm, 35mm, 50mm, 85mm, 200mm)
- Aperture (f/1.4, f/2.8, f/4, f/8)
- Depth of field (shallow, deep)
- Perspective (low angle, high angle, eye level, aerial)

### Composition
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines
- Symmetry
- Frame within frame
- Negative space

## Prompt Generator Function (for LLM)

When generating prompts from lyrics, use this structure:

```
ANALYZE:
- Song title: {title}
- Genre: {genre}
- Section type: {verse/chorus/bridge}
- Lyrics content: {lyrics}

EXTRACT:
- Key imagery and visual metaphors
- Emotional tone
- Time of day implied
- Location hints
- Colors mentioned or implied

GENERATE:
- Scene description matching lyrical imagery
- Appropriate location
- Lighting matching mood and time
- Color palette matching emotion
- Cinematic camera work
- Professional quality modifiers
- Complete negative prompt

OUTPUT: Single detailed prompt following master template
```
